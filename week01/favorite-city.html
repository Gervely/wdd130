My Favorite City

My favorite city is Brazzaville. I love its beautiful architecture, rich history, and vibrant culture

Brazzaville is the capital of the Republic of Congo and is located on the Congo River, directly across from Kinshasa, the capital of the Democratic Republic of Congo. The city is known for its colonial-era buildings, bustling markets, and lively music scene.
